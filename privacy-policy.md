## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **ArcHandler** (“we,” “our,” or “us”).  
We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through this Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the ArcHandler mobile application (the “App”).

---

## 2. Information We Collect

**ArcHandler does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

The App works entirely offline and processes files locally on your device.

We do **not** collect:

- Personal information
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics
- Crash reports
- Advertising data

---

## 3. Local File Access

ArcHandler may request permission to access files on your device **only to provide core functionality**, such as:

- Opening archives
- Creating archives
- Managing local files

All file processing happens **locally on your device** and is **not uploaded** to any server.

We do not access files beyond what is necessary for the App’s functionality.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

ArcHandler does **not** use any third-party services that collect user data.

Specifically, the App does not integrate:

- Advertising SDKs
- Analytics services
- Cloud services
- Remote servers

---

## 5. Data Sharing

Because we do not collect user data, we do **not** sell, trade, or share personal information with any third parties.

---

## 6. Data Security

Since ArcHandler does not transmit or store personal data externally, your information remains on your device under your control.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

ArcHandler does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Because the App does not collect data, it is safe for general audiences.
